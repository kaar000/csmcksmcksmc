The Journey
Behind Our
Blog

Our travel blog began as a personal diary—filled with stories of getting lost in unfamiliar streets,
discovering hidden gems, and experiencing cultures that left a lasting impact. What started as a way
to document our adventures soon turned into a passion for sharing travel tips, itineraries, and inspiring
others to explore the world.

One unforgettable moment that changed our perspective was diving in the crystal-clear waters of Camiguin,
Philippines, where vibrant coral reefs and marine life reminded us of the beauty beneath the surface. As
we explored White Island, we felt the magic of Mindanao—untouched, diverse, and full of adventure waiting
to be discovered.

Our Goal

Wanderlust's vision is to empower travelers to explore the world with confidence and curiosity. We achieve this
by providing insightful travel resources, budget-friendly advice, and personal travel stories that inspire
unforgettable experiences. Our goal is to make travel more accessible, meaningful, and enriching for all.

Our Favorite Destination

White Island, Camiguin, Philippines

A stunning, ever-changing sandbar with powdery white sand and turquoise waters, this little paradise is a must-visit. We love it for its unspoiled beauty, breathtaking sunrise views, and the dramatic backdrop of Mt. Hibok-Hibok. Whether you're here for a relaxing swim or simply to take in the serene surroundings, White Island is a true escape into nature.

Myeongdong, Seoul, South Korea

A bustling shopping and street food paradise in the heart of Seoul, Myeongdong is where K-beauty, fashion, and delicious local eats come together. We love it for its energy, neon-lit streets, and endless discoveries—from the latest skincare trends to sizzling tteokbokki and crispy hotteok. Whether you're a foodie, a shopaholic, or just soaking in the K-drama vibes, Myeongdong is a must-visit

Florence, Tuscany, Italy

This destination holds a special place for us because Florence embodies a perfect harmony of history, art, and culture. From exploring the renowned Uffizi Gallery to admiring the brilliance of Michelangelo’s David, every corner of the city feels like a living masterpiece. Its cobblestone streets, lively markets, and exquisite Italian cuisine create an atmosphere of timeless elegance that continues to captivate us
